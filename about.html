About TruePrice

TruePrice is a price comparison platform designed to help users find the best available deals across trusted online shopping platforms.

Instead of manually searching on multiple websites, TruePrice simplifies the process by redirecting users to relevant product listings, saving both time and effort.

Transparency and trust are our priorities. Some links on this website may be affiliate links, which means we may earn a small commission if a purchase is made â€” at no extra cost to the user.

TruePrice does not sell products directly. We only provide verified redirections to partner platforms to help users make informed shopping decisions.

TruePrice is built with the goal of helping users compare prices across platforms. At present, the platform redirects users to trusted partner websites while we continue refining advanced comparison features.

TruePrice is independently conceptualized, designed, and developed by Piyush Ahuja with a focus on building a transparent and user-friendly price comparison platform.
