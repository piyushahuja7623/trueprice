Privacy Policy

At TruePrice, your privacy is respected. This website is designed to provide a simple and transparent price comparison experience without collecting personal user information.

Information We Do Not Collect

Affiliate Links

TruePrice contains affiliate links to third-party shopping platforms. When you click these links, you are redirected to external websites that operate under their own privacy policies. TruePrice does not control or store data from these platforms.

Cookies

TruePrice does not intentionally use cookies to track users. Any cookies used are controlled by third-party services such as analytics or affiliate platforms.

Third-Party Websites

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites linked from TruePrice. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of third-party platforms before making any purchase.
